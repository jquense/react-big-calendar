# rtl

- type: `boolean`
- default: `false`

Switch the calendar to a right-to-left read direction. Best if used in conjunction with your <LinkTo kind="props" story="localizer">localizer</LinkTo> and <LinkTo kind="props" story="messages">messages</LinkTo> props.

See the <LinkTo kind="examples" story="example-5">Localization Example</LinkTo> for another example of combining `rtl`, `localizer`, `culture` and `messages`.
